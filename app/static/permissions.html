Permissions

The descendants of Alfred and Emily Tennyson, and of Mary Gladstone Drew, give permission to display items on Sounding Tennyson. The British Library and The Huntington Library agree to the display of their archival objects on Sounding Tennyson, and to the hosting by the Center for Digital Humanities at Saint Louis University (CDH). The Lincolnshire County Council agrees to their archival materials being hosted by CDH and Cambridge Digital Library. All of the above require appropriate acknowledgments of their copyright, with which we are happy to comply.
